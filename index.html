Code of Conduct

Procedure

Life Of PY will be an event comprising of a team of 2 people, and the resigstrations will be open for all category with no prequistes from the participants.It will contain a total of 3-5 proble statements from different genre of Python which will be available to all the participants from start of the event.Anyone can start solving in any order and submit multiple solutions.

Scoring Pattern

Each problem statement will have its own requirement for scoring and submisiions will be judged on the same basis.The most optimized submission will be given highest marks followed by subsequent optimized submissions.The final scoreboard will be prepared from collective scores in problems solved by the team.From the final scoreboard,top 10 teams will be eligible for he prizes.
